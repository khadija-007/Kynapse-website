Our Journey

KYNAPSE was founded in 2024 during the post-pandemic educational transformation, when technology became the heart of learning. With the rise of artificial intelligence, our founder envisioned a platform that could bridge the gap between human mentorship and smart technology. Today, AI Tutors proudly serves over one million learners worldwide, offering flexible and personalized education for every student.
